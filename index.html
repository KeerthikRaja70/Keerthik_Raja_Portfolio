Profile Summary

Bridging Industrial Systems & Analytics

My professional journey began in Industrial Engineering, where my days revolved around production lines, resource distribution audits, and identifying process bottlenecks. I quickly learned that physical bottlenecks are solved by structuring and understanding transaction logs. That realization prompted my transition into Data Analytics.

I specialize in building query architectures (joins, CTEs, aggregates) in SQL, modeling databases via DAX inside Power BI, and scripting wrangling solutions in Python (Pandas/NumPy). I convert millions of rows of messy records into interactive, responsive dashboards that immediately drive operational efficiency, close forecasting gaps, and optimize inventory allocation.
